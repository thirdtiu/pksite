To Build An Empire,
You Must First Lose Everything

My name is Paul Klebanov. As marketing educator and speaker at SEMrush, I take away the mystery of marketing and replace it with knowledge; those little “a-ha!” moments that gift audiences with crystal-clear clarity. How am I able to do that? I am one of the youngest Americans to build a leading ecommerce retailer - PPL Stores LLC. My goal was to make $1 million in revenue before 30…

I actually made over $3.1 million by 27. And then I lost it all.

The knowledge gained from years of hardship and experience coupled with my burning desire to get it all back developed into a range of online business specializations and battle-tested digital marketing strategies that continue to fuel my clients business and keynote presentation topics.
